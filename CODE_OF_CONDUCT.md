# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Use respectful and professional language.
- Assume good intent and collaborate constructively.
- Focus on technical issues and user impact.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate intimidation, trolling, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private data without explicit consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, or access for conduct violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through repository support channels listed in `SUPPORT` resources.
